Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about admissions, campus, and academics.

How can I apply for admission?

Visit our Admissions page on the official website or contact our admissions helpline at +91 758 857 1100. Applications are accepted through CAP rounds and direct admission based on merit.

What are the eligibility criteria for B.Tech?

Candidates must have passed HSC (10+2) with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects, along with one of Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Technical subjects with at least 45% marks (40% for reserved categories). JEE Main or MHT-CET scores are considered.

Does WIT provide hostel facilities?

Yes, separate hostel facilities are available for boys and girls with modern amenities, mess facilities, and 24/7 security. Hostel admission is based on merit and availability.

What is the placement record of WIT?

WIT has an excellent placement record with over 60+ companies visiting annually. The highest on-campus package is ₹12.5 LPA, with a median package of ₹5.10 LPA. Our placement rate has been consistently growing year-over-year.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, scholarships are available for eligible students including EBC, SEBC, Minority, and Reserve Category scholarships. AICTE scholarship/fellowship schemes are also available. Contact the administration office for more details.
