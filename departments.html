Civil Engineering

Est. 1987 | Intake: 60
NBA Accredited B.Tech M.Tech Ph.D.

B.Tech with Honors in Infrastructure Engineering or Sustainability Engineering. M.Tech in Structural Engineering. Ph.D. programs in advanced construction materials, structural analysis, and environmental engineering. NBA accredited with strong industry partnerships and hands-on laboratory experience.

Computer Science & Engineering

Est. 1985 | Intake: 180
NBA Accredited B.Tech M.Tech Ph.D.

M.Tech (2009-10) and Ph.D. through PAHSU-recognized research centre. Curriculum spans AI/ML, data science, distributed systems, network security, algorithms, and software engineering. 10 advanced labs with GPU-enabled workstations for deep learning research.

Electronics & Computer Engineering

Est. 1983 | Intake: 60
NBA Accredited B.Tech M.Tech Ph.D.

M.Tech (2009-10) with doctoral research centre. Specializes in embedded systems, VLSI design, IoT architecture, and the convergence of hardware and software engineering. Strong foundation in both electronics and computing with modern lab facilities.

Electronics & Telecommunication

Est. 1999 | Intake: 120
NBA Accredited B.Tech

Evolved from 40 seats (1999) to 120 seats today. Core focus on signal processing, communication systems, wireless technology, IoT, embedded systems, and 5G/6G communication technologies. Well-equipped labs for telecom and RF engineering.

Information Technology

Est. 1999 | Intake: 120
NBA Accredited B.Tech

10 fully-equipped computer labs with licensed software across networking, multimedia, databases, and mobile app development. Curriculum integrates AI/ML, data science, cloud computing, and cybersecurity with hands-on project-based learning.

Mechanical & Automation Engineering

Est. 1983 | Intake: 60
NBA Accredited B.Tech M.Tech Ph.D.

AICTE-CII recognized for industry collaboration. Offers M.Tech in Design Engineering and Ph.D. programs. Strong placement record with consultancy services, sponsored research projects, and infrastructure sharing partnerships with leading industries.
