Study @ WIT

Explore our diverse undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs.

All Programs
CSE

Computer Science & Engineering

Intake: 180

Est. 1985 with UG course; PG (M.Tech) started in 2009-10. Recognized research centre of PAHSU. Curriculum covers programming, networking, algorithms, software engineering, distributed systems, network security, data mining, big data analytics and AI/ML. Runs Honors in AI & ML and Data Science.
ENTC

Electronics & Telecommunication

Intake: 120

Est. 1999 with intake 40, now 120 seats. Offers Honors in IoT, Data Science and AI & ML. 12 major labs including Audio/Video, Microwave, Communication, IoT and Signal Processing. Recognized research centre for doctoral studies.

ME

Mechanical & Automation Engineering

Intake: 60

AICTE-CII recognized Best Industry Linked Institute. NBA re-accredited UG (intake 60) and PG in Design Engineering. Focus on automation, 3D printing, Industry 4.0, AI/ML and digital twin technologies.

CE

Civil Engineering

Intake: 60

Est. 1987. B.Tech with Honors in Infrastructure or Sustainability Engineering. M.Tech in Structural Engineering and Ph.D. NBA accredited 4 times. National Award from AICTE-CII. 13 labs with ₹1.2 Cr in research grants.

IT

Information Technology

Intake: 120

Est. 1999. 10 computer labs with latest software and licensed copies. Specializes in AI/ML, data science, cloud computing, cybersecurity, mobile app development and networking. MOUs with IBM, Cognizant and Infosys.

ECM

Electronics & Computer Engineering

Intake: 60

Est. 1983; PG from 2009-10. Recognized for doctoral research. Renamed to Electronics & Computer Engineering in 2021-22. Focus on embedded systems, VLSI, IoT, ML and data analytics. Sponsored labs by Texas Instruments, Cypress and ARM.
