From the Principal's Desk

At Walchand Institute of Technology, Solapur, we believe in nurturing not just engineers — but innovators, leaders, and responsible citizens. Our commitment to academic excellence, research, and holistic development has positioned WIT as one of the premier engineering institutions in the region.

With NAAC A+ accreditation, NBA-accredited programs, and a dedicated faculty committed to mentoring, WIT offers an environment where students thrive. Our state-of-the-art laboratories, innovation cell, and strong industry partnerships ensure that our graduates are industry-ready from day one.

I invite you to explore our campus, interact with our community, and experience the WIT difference. Together, let us build a future of excellence.

Prof. (Dr.) P. N. Athavale

Principal, Walchand Institute of Technology, Solapur
