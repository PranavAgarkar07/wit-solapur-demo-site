About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at WIT Solapur is one of the most sought-after departments, established in 1985. It offers a comprehensive UG program (B.Tech) and a PG program (M.Tech started in 2009-10), along with Doctoral research programs.

The department's research laboratory is a recognized research centre of Punyashlok Ahilyadevi Holkar Solapur University (PAHSU), contributing significantly to the advancement of computing knowledge.

The academic curriculum covers a wide spectrum of subjects — computer programming, networking, algorithms, theory of computation, software engineering, distributed systems, network security, modern databases, data mining, big data analytics, artificial intelligence and machine learning.

With an intake of 180 students and a dedicated faculty of experienced scholars, the department maintains excellent academic records with consistent university ranks and gold medalists.
